Introduction

Welcome to Defendra. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website Defendra, use our services, or interact with us.

Information We Collect
Your data is used for the following purposes:

Personal Information: When you sign up, contact us, or interact with our website, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other personal details.
Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our site. For more information, please see our Cookies Policy.
How We Use Your Information
We collect information in the following ways:

To provide, operate, and maintain our website and services
To improve and personalize your experience on our site
To process your transactions and respond to your requests
To send you updates, promotions, and other marketing information, with your consent
To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted partners who help us provide services, such as payment processors, email service providers, or analytics tools, but only to the extent necessary to perform their functions. We ensure that these partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
Correction: You have the right to request that we correct or update your personal data.
Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data under certain circumstances.
Withdraw Consent: If we are processing your data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
Security of Your Information

We take security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no security measure is completely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Retention of Data

We will retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it or to comply with legal obligations.

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing them with any personal data.
