About Us

Our Mission

At Defendra, our mission is to provide top-tier home and business security systems that ensure the safety and peace of mind of our clients in Hampshire and Greater London. We are committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and innovative security services that protect your home and business.

Our Story

Founded at the start of 2024, Defendra emerged from a passion for security and a commitment to excellence. Our founder, Werick Passon, started the company after a personal experience highlighted the need for reliable security solutions. Over time, we have grown to become a trusted name in security solutions, known for our professionalism and dedication to client satisfaction.

Our dedicated team ensuring your safety.
